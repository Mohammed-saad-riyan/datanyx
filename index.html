Oct 21 - Nov 5 Register

Registration + Problem Submission

Teams must complete the official registration form by 5th November to secure their participation in Datanyx. A shortlisting process will follow, where certain teams will be selected to move forward. In this phase, teams are required to submit a unique problem statement of their own creation, accompanied by a solution abstract that aligns with their chosen domain. The available domains for selection are MedTech, FinTech, AgroTech, or EdTech.

Nov 16th & 17th Datathon

Datanyx Begins Here

The top 15 teams from each domain will qualify for the final phase, held in person at MJCET, Hyderabad. Here, teams will face a fresh problem statement revealed on the spot. They will then have 24 hours to brainstorm, design, and submit their final solutions. This phase tests the teams' ability to think critically and collaborate effectively under pressure, turning ideas into impactful solutions within a limited timeframe.

FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

What is Datanyx?

Datanyx'24 is a 24-hour datathon organized by IEEE SMC MJCET and AWS Cloud Club MJCET,where teams use their expertise in machine learning, data analysis, and creativity to submit innovative solutions.

01

What are the eligibility criteria for participating in Datanyx?

Participants must be undergraduate students from MJCET, forming teams of 2 to 4 members.

02

How do we register for Datanyx?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the register button here and filling up a google form in which you would have to mention you and your team details.

03

What are the available domains for problem statements in Datanyx?

Participants can choose from the following domains for their problem statements: MedTech, FinTech, AgroTech, or EdTech.

04

Is there a participation fee?

While registration for Datanyx is completely free, a small participation fee of 400 INR per team will be required if your team is shortlisted for the event.

05

Can we submit more than one problem statement for Datanyx?

Teams are allowed to submit only one problem statement for Datanyx. Multiple entries from the same team are not permitted.
